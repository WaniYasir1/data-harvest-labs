What We Do

Core Analytical Services

Rigorous, reproducible genomic analysis pipelines tailored to your species, data type, and research objectives.

🧬

Genomic Diversity Analysis

Population structure (ADMIXTURE, STRUCTURE), PCA, nucleotide diversity (π, Tajima's D), Fst, kinship matrices, ROH, and phylogenetics.

PCA ADMIXTURE Fst / π Kinship ROH
🔬

SNP Data Analysis

Genotype QC & filtering (MAF, HWE), imputation (Beagle/MaCH), LD analysis, haplotype phasing, and functional variant annotation.

QC / Filtering Imputation LD Analysis Annotation
📊

GWAS – Linear & Mixed Models

Genome-wide association using LMM, EMMAX, BLINK, FarmCPU, MLMM, and GLM — controlling for population structure and kinship.

LMM EMMAX FarmCPU BLINK MLMM
📐

Heritability Estimation

Narrow- and broad-sense heritability, genomic heritability (GREML/GBLUP), variance component decomposition, and bivariate models.

GREML GBLUP BayesA/B/C h²g
📈

ANOVA & Regression Analysis

One-way & two-way ANOVA, multi-environment trials, repeated measures, post-hoc tests, linear & logistic regression, LASSO/Ridge models.

MET MANOVA Regression Ridge/LASSO

Expert Genomic Analysis, Start to Finish
